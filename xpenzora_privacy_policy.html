Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Xpenzora is a business expense and sales management application designed for business owners and individuals. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your email address and password (for login via Firebase Authentication).
  • Business Information: Business name, currency symbol, and location (entered manually as text).
  • Transactions & Images: Sales and expenses you record, along with uploaded images (such as bills, receipts, or profile pictures).

3. Permissions Required

  • Camera / Gallery: To capture or upload images of bills, receipts, or profile photos.
  • Internet: To connect securely with Firebase services.

We do not request or use location permissions, contacts, microphone, or any other personal device data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account securely.
  • To store and display your businesses, expenses, and sales.
  • To allow you to upload and view receipts and profile photos.
  • To support team collaboration (owners, managers, employees) with different access levels.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). Images are stored in Firebase Storage. We use standard security practices to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is only shared within your team (owner, manager, employee) depending on assigned roles.

7. User Rights

You can update or delete your data within the app. You can request permanent deletion of your account by contacting us at [your email].

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will always be available at this link.
